##### Ethics
* Actually goes into Philosophical Schools of Thought
    * Teleological Ethics
        * Egoism: Act to help youself
        * Utilitarianism: Act to help the greater good
    * Deontological Ethics
        * Focused on duties, rules and principles (rather than outcomes)
    * Contractarianism
        * Focuses on rules and principles that people *would* agree to as part of a social contract for living fairly
    * Ethical Pluralism
        * There is not always one ethical rule or correct way to judge a situation (we need multiple values to matter at one time)
